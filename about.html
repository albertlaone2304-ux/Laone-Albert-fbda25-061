About Blue Tide

How We Started

Our Story

Blue Tide opened its doors with a simple idea: seafood tastes best when it barely travels. We started as a small harbour-side stall serving grilled fish to fishermen and dock workers, and grew into a full restaurant without ever losing that early-morning, fresh-off-the-boat spirit.

Today, the same relationships with local fishing crews that started it all still shape our menu — what comes in on the boats each morning decides what goes on the specials board that night.

Our Mission

To serve honest, fresh seafood in a warm setting, prepared simply enough to let the quality of the catch speak for itself.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted seafood table — known as much for sustainable sourcing as for what's on the plate.

Meet the Chef

Chef Kabo Sedimo

Chef Kabo trained in coastal kitchens before bringing that experience home to open Blue Tide's kitchen. His approach favours restraint over complication — a handful of precise techniques applied to the best ingredient he can find that day.

"The fish does most of the work. My job is to not get in its way."

Our Commitment

Fresh Ingredients, Every Day

We work directly with a small group of local suppliers who share our standards. Nothing sits in cold storage longer than it has to — most of what's served tonight was still in the water this morning.

Looking After the Ocean

Sustainability

  • We only serve species from healthy, well-managed fisheries.
  • Kitchen waste is composted and packaging is kept plastic-light.
  • We rotate our menu with the seasons instead of over-fishing any one species.
