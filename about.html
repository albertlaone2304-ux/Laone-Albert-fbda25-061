About Blue Tide

How We Started

Our Story

Blue Tide opened with a simple idea: seafood tastes best when it barely travels. We grew from a small harbour-side stall into a full restaurant, without losing that fresh-off-the-boat spirit.

The same relationships with local fishing crews that started it all still shape our menu today — what comes in on the boats each morning decides tonight's specials.

Our Mission

Honest, fresh seafood in a warm setting — simply prepared so the quality of the catch speaks for itself.

Our Vision

To be the region's most trusted seafood table, known for sustainable sourcing as much as for what's on the plate.

From Our Kitchen

Restraint Over Complication

Our kitchen favours a handful of precise techniques applied to the best ingredient we can find that day.

"The fish does most of the work. Our job is to not get in its way."

Our Commitment

Fresh Ingredients, Every Day

We work directly with a small group of local suppliers. Nothing sits in cold storage longer than it has to.

Looking After the Ocean

Sustainability

  • We only serve species from healthy, well-managed fisheries.
  • Kitchen waste is composted; packaging is kept plastic-light.
  • Our menu rotates with the seasons rather than over-fishing any one species.
